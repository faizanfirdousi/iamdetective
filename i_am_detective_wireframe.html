United States v. Samuel Bankman-Fried
CLOSED FINANCIAL FRAUD COURTLISTENER
Court: SDNY Filed: DEC 12, 2022 Terminated: NOV 02, 2023 Jurisdiction: Federal
▸ CASE OVERVIEW
Samuel Bankman-Fried, founder of cryptocurrency exchange FTX, was charged with seven counts of wire fraud, securities fraud, commodities fraud, and money laundering conspiracy. Federal prosecutors alleged that Bankman-Fried secretly used billions of dollars of FTX customer funds to support his hedge fund Alameda Research, cover losses, and finance personal investments. The scheme resulted in approximately $8 billion in customer losses.
